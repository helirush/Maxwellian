# Unity Energy - Full Analysis Button Structure

**Version:** 2.0  
**Date:** February 1, 2026  
**Component:** Summary Board - Transformer Analysis Modal

---

## Overview
The "FULL ANALYSIS" button on each transformer card opens a comprehensive three-part narrative that tells the complete story: the problem, the solution, and the results. This creates a persuasive, educational customer experience backed by data visualization.

---

## Purpose
- **Educate** customers about their electrical inefficiencies
- **Quantify** waste and cost impact with real data
- **Present** clear solution with equipment, cost, timeline
- **Project** savings and ROI after Unity MPTS installation
- **Build confidence** in Unity's approach through detailed analysis

---

## Three-Part Narrative Structure

### PART 1: THE PROBLEM (Baseline Analysis)
**Visual:** Baseline Pattern Image (BT - "Baseline Thermal")  
**Narrator:** Unity (voice: Shimmer)

#### Content Focus
1. **Current State Description**
   - Describe the transformer's current electrical behavior
   - Identify inefficiency patterns in the energy field
   - Explain what "unharmonized" means in customer terms

2. **Waste Quantification**
   - Reactive power waste (kVAR)
   - Power factor inefficiency (%)
   - Wasted energy converted to heat (kW)
   - Thermal burden entering the system (BTUs)

3. **Cost Impact**
   - Current monthly/annual energy waste cost
   - Current monthly/annual cooling cost to remove heat
   - Total economic impact of inefficiency

4. **Operational Impact**
   - Production cost per unit (e.g., cost per bird)
   - Energy per unit (e.g., Watts per bird)
   - Equipment stress and longevity concerns

   - **Read** the text at their own pace
   - **Click** "Unity Explains" to hear audio narration
   - **Click** images to view full-screen
   - **Scroll** through the three-part story

1. Analyst reviews pattern data

1. **William (GPT-5.2)** analyzes pattern data after eeBEHAVIOR.py generates study

   - Baseline pattern CSV and image
   - Transformer specifications and metrics
   - MPTS simulation results
   - Cost calculations

5. **eAudio** processes text with OpenAI TTS-HD1 "Shimmer" voice

## Writing Guidelines for William

### Tone
- **Professional** but accessible (8th-10th grade reading level)
- **Confident** in technical accuracy
- **Educational** without being condescending
- **Persuasive** through data, not marketing hype

### Structure
- Use **short paragraphs** (2-4 sentences each)
- Include **specific numbers** (no vague "significant" without quantification)
- Use **customer's language** (e.g., "birds" for poultry, "tons" for cooling)
- **Compare** before/after directly with parallel structure

### Technical Accuracy
- All metrics must match dashboard data exactly
- Calculations must be verifiable from source CSVs
- ROI projections based on actual utility rates and measurements
- No promises that can't be demonstrated

### Prohibited
- Exaggeration or hyperbole
- Unfounded environmental claims
- Competitor bashing
- Legal/regulatory advice
